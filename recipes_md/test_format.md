## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients
- 2 cups **flour**, *sifted*
- 1 tbsp ***butter*** (melted)
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt

### Wet Ingredients
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup [olive oil](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olive_oil)

### Spices & Seasonings
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375{{unit:degF}} (190{{unit:degC}}).
2. **Mix dry ingredients:** Combine flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
3. *Whisk wet ingredients:* Beat eggs, then add milk and olive oil.
4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until ***just*** mixed.
5. Pour batter into greased pan.
6. Bake for 25-30 minutes at 375{{unit:deg}}.
7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

---

## Notes

### Tips
- Do not overmix the batter - lumps are okay!
- For best results, use room temperature ingredients.
- Store in an airtight container.

### Variations
- **Chocolate version:** Add 1/4 cup cocoa powder.
- *Citrus twist:* Add 1 tbsp lemon zest.
- ***Double indulgence:*** Add both chocolate chips and walnuts.

### Storage
- Room temperature: 3-4 days
- Refrigerated: 1 week
- Frozen: Up to 3 months
